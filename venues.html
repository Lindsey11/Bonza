CONFERENCE VENUES

SAGE

Welcome to the Sage Room, the epitome of luxury and sophistication. With its tastefully designed boardroom-style setting, this exclusive conference room is perfect for high-level meetings. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and plush seating for 8/10 delegates, indulge in the ambiance of elegance and cultivate success in unparalleled comfort.

FERN

Introducing the Fern Room – An innovative conference space that combines functionality with inspiration. Equipped with an interactive, touch-screen TV – this room revolutionizes conferences by transforming a typical conference into an engaging and interactive experience. Seating between 10-12 delegates in a board room style and up to 20 delegates in classroom seating, it is perfect for productive meeting.

OLIVE

This space offers a discreet and secluded setting for your board meetings and private gatherings. Situated away from the main areas, the Olive Room provides the perfect escape for those seeking a more intimate and confidential atmosphere. With its prime location on the upper floor, you can enjoy peace and quite while conducting your business affairs. The Olive Room is designed to accommodate your boardroom needs, ensuring a professional and focused environment for your important discussions and decision-making

JUNIPER

Immerse yourself in the elegance of the Juniper Room – a versatile space perfect for hosting intimate gatherings or corporate meetings. A luxurious and adaptable venue designed to comfortably accommodate 24 delegates in a school room configuration. With its unique breakaway room, this space offers privacy and flexibility for smaller discussions or private meetings. Experience a seamless blend of sophistication and convenience in this exclusive setting.
